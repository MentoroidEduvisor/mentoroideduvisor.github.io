What is Mentoroid?

Mentoroid Eduvisor acts as a mentor and growth partner for students. We guide students to achieve their dream job, dream profession, and financial independence. Mentoroid helps students succeed by doing the right things at the right time. We believe learning and earning should go hand in hand. Mentoroid is a solution for every challenge faced during the journey of success.

Why Mentoroid is Required?

India has millions of unemployed youth not because jobs don’t exist, but because skills and guidance are missing. Many students have potential but fall behind due to lack of direction. Mentoroid bridges this gap by providing skill-based learning, real-world exposure, and personalized mentorship.
