What is Mentoroid?

Mentoroid guides students to achieve their dreams while learning and earning. Our platform provides personalized mentorship, AI-powered guidance, and real-world opportunities for students to grow their careers.

Why Mentoroid is Required?

Skill Development

Learn social media marketing, freelancing, and other skills to become industry-ready.

Personalized Guidance

Each student receives mentorship tailored to their career path and field.

AI Assistance

Mentoroid AI helps students with study guidance, work tips, and project support.

Earn While Learning

Collaborate with brands, get freelancing projects, and build a professional portfolio.

Our Vision

Mentoroid is creating a culture of freelancers and helping students explore multiple ways to learn and earn. Our upcoming app will allow students to learn skills, get clients for freelancing projects, collaborate with brands, and showcase their portfolios — all in one place.
