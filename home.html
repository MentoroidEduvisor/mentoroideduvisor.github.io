What is Mentoroid?

Mentoroid Eduvisor is your personal mentor and growth partner. We guide students to achieve their dreams — landing their dream job, mastering skills, or building a successful freelancing career. Mentoroid shows you how to succeed by doing the right things at the right time, step by step. We help students gain financial independence even while learning and encourage them to follow their passion to earn. Mentoroid is not just a platform; it’s your partner in learning, earning, and growing — helping you overcome challenges and unlock your full potential.

Why Mentoroid is Required?

Millions of youth in India are unemployed — not because jobs are missing, but because they lack skills. Companies struggle to find qualified candidates, while many talented students fall behind due to a lack of guidance. Mentoroid bridges this gap by providing skill-based training, personalized mentorship, and real-world guidance. We prepare students for MNCs, freelancing projects, and skill-based professions that truly pay. Mentoroid empowers students to learn practical skills, discover earning opportunities, and become confident, industry-ready professionals.

Our Vision

Mentoroid’s vision is to create a system where every student receives quality education and guidance to achieve their dream job or profession. We ensure everyone can do the work they love. Mentoroid makes learning practical, not just theoretical, focusing on skills that truly pay. Mentoroid ensures your journey from student to professional is smooth, supported, and successful.
